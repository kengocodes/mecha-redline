# Neo-Kyoto Garrison — Prototype Flight

Playable roster for **MECHA REDLINE**. Gear callsign is primary; the pilot is the face in the hangar CRT still.

Art lives under `public/portraits/` (pilot stills) and `public/gears/` (full-body gear plates). Magenta backgrounds are chroma-key plates.

---

## 01 · VALKYR

| | |
| --- | --- |
| **Pilot** | Kira Ash |
| **Role** | Standard interceptor |
| **Portrait** | `public/portraits/valkyr-kira-ash.png` |
| **Gear plate** | `public/gears/valkyr.png` |

### Personality

Calm professional. Has run the redline enough times that fear reads as checklist. Speaks little on comms; when she does, it is short and useful. Trusts the plan, trusts the wing, does not grandstand. The flight’s center of gravity.

### Doctrine

Balanced all-rounder. Readable movement, forgiving armor, reliable rifle. The mission default — teach the game, then compete on execution.

### Loadout (target feel)

- Twin-wing binders, visor head, antenna fins
- Right-hand rifle
- 4 armor · mid speed · 3 Burst
- Indigo plating, crimson accent, silver trim, cyan optic

### Portrait read

Cool composure under cyan cockpit light. Not a scowl — quiet certainty.

## 02 · RAVEN

| | |
| --- | --- |
| **Pilot** | Ren Okada |
| **Role** | Glass-cannon skirmisher |
| **Portrait** | `public/portraits/raven-ren-okada.png` |
| **Gear plate** | `public/gears/raven.png` |

### Personality

Cocky hotshot. Treats sorties like dares. Smirks when the operator says “hold formation.” Not cruel — just restless, competitive, and convinced the shortest path through a wave is through it. Will apologize after the explosion, not before.

### Doctrine

High-mobility knife fight. Get in, empty the mag, get out. Less armor, less Burst, more speed and denser fire. Punishes hesitation; punishes the pilot who panics.

### Loadout (target feel)

- Lean frame, no wing binders
- Fast rifle / tight focus
- 3 armor · high speed · 2 Burst · thinner hitbox
- Charcoal plating, amber trim, amber cockpit wash

### Portrait read

Crooked smirk, undercut, brow scar. Looks like he already decided to ignore the briefing.

## 03 · IVORY

| | |
| --- | --- |
| **Pilot** | Sera Vale |
| **Role** | Focus sniper |
| **Portrait** | `public/portraits/ivory-sera-vale.png` |
| **Gear plate** | `public/gears/ivory.png` |

### Personality

Distant and serene. Not cold — just elsewhere until the shot matters. Listens more than she talks. On the channel she sounds almost gentle, which unsettles people who expect sniper arrogance. Precision is how she cares.

### Doctrine

Owns the Focus lane. Normal fire is polite; Focus is a needle. Rewards aim and patience. Slower on the strafe, lethal when the reticle settles.

### Loadout (target feel)

- Mono optic, long rifle, pale silhouette
- 4 armor · slow move · 3 Burst · near-zero focus spread
- Bone / silver-white plating, cool optic glow

### Portrait read

Faraway gaze, soft stillness. Melancholy quiet, not a stern glare.

## 04 · BASALT

| | |
| --- | --- |
| **Pilot** | Juno “Brick” Hale |
| **Role** | Bruiser / board clearer |
| **Portrait** | `public/portraits/basalt-juno-hale.png` |
| **Gear plate** | `public/gears/basalt.png` |

### Personality

Warm bruiser. Loud in the hangar, steady in the cockpit. Calls people by nicknames they did not ask for. Believes armor is a personality trait. After the mission she is the one buying the first round and checking who needs a hand off the gantry.

### Doctrine

Walk the redline. Trade hits. Clear the board. Slow, thick, Burst-rich. Bad at dancing; good at ending arguments.

### Loadout (target feel)

- Bulk frame, arm cannon, heavy pauldrons
- 5 armor · slow · 4 Burst · wide spread · larger hitbox
- Iron / rust / bone accents, warm exhaust glow

### Portrait read

Headset crushed into messy hair, solid presence. Approachable toughness — the face of someone who will hold the line and then laugh about it.

## Select-screen flow (intended)

**Title → Hangar Select → Mission**

1. Gear plate or in-engine preview of the selected unit.
2. Roster strip `01–04` with callsign + pilot name.
3. CRT portrait panel (keyed still + scanlines).
4. Doctrine line + armor / speed / Burst bars in existing HUD language.
